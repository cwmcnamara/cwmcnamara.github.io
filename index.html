About Me

My Shot, My Goal

Alot of things make up a person, the same can be said about most things. I am not the rose that grew from the concrete. But I am the railroad spike that would not be nailed down. I am the voice that can not be quieted even when everyone else remains silent. I am determined to change the world. I believe that I have the skills necessary to invent revolutionary technologies or to spread messages of change that ripple through every country. What skills do I have then? I am a programmer, a developer. I have all the ideas and I can write code efficiently and effectively so now I just need the education to help me connect the dots. I said that I am not the rose that grew from the concrete, but I will make sure that those who are the roses bloom and that we celebrate their tenacity.
